Welcome

Hello. Welcome to my website! I am a PhD student in the Department of Economic Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (IITK). My broad research area is Applied Microeconomics. Currently, I am studying how exposure to different forms of violence ranging from armed conflicts, civil unrests and terrorism affect human capital formation, fertility outcomes and family composition. I obtained my M.A. in Economics from the Department of Economics at Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), New Delhi, in 2019. I completed my B. A. (Hons.) in Economics from the Department of Economics at Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), Aligarh U.P, in 2017.
